Welcome to My Website!

Hi stranger,

Welcome to my little corner of the web. Here, I share some of my explorations at the crossroads of education and artificial intelligence, a passion that has grown over the years as I dived into the world of natural language processing and machine learning. As a former history teacher, I became captivated by how education evolves and shapes society, and lately, I’ve been fascinated by how AI can transform teaching and learning.

Beyond AI and education, I have a keen interest in philosophy (Kant!) and cosmology (Quantum-Physics)—marveling, when time befits, on the wonders of life and the universe. I’m also drawn to the arts, photography, travel, and history. And it took me until my forties to discover I actually prefer dogs over cats. A worthy topics for LLM's to disagree about!

I created this site to document and share some of my journey. Whether it’s electronic literature, seniors basketball, or something else entirely, I hope you’ll find something here that inspires you to keep exploring.
